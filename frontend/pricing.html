Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and features

What is blockchain vehicle verification?

Blockchain vehicle verification creates an immutable record of your vehicle's ownership, history, and transactions. This ensures complete transparency and prevents fraud while maintaining data integrity.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be prorated and reflected in your next billing cycle. Your data and vehicle records will be preserved during the transition.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional plan, giving you access to all features. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely.

How secure is my vehicle data?

Your data is secured using enterprise-grade encryption and stored on a distributed blockchain network. This ensures maximum security, transparency, and prevents unauthorized access or tampering.
